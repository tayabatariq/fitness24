Steady State Cardio

The Foundation of Endurance & Recovery

Low to moderate intensity cardio performed for 30–60 minutes is highly effective for fat oxidation, active recovery, and improving your aerobic base. It's the ideal complement to your weight training sessions and HIIT work.

  • Treadmill Incline Walking

    Walking at 6–8% incline burns 3x more calories than flat walking — a highly underrated fat loss tool.

  • Stationary Cycling

    Low impact, joint-friendly option for sustained fat burning over longer durations.

  • Rowing Machine

    Full body cardio that also builds upper back strength — one machine, maximum benefit.
